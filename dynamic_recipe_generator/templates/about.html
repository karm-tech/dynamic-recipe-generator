🚀 What We Built in DRG 4.0

With DRG 4.0, users can:

  • Search for recipes by ingredient or name
  • Download recipes as PDFs
  • Get auto-generated shopping lists
  • View smart accessory suggestions
  • Explore recipes based on seasonal festivals

🔄 What’s Coming in DRG 5.0

  • 🗣️ Voice-Activated Cooking Assistant – Hands-free control with voice commands.
  • 🔐 User Login System – Personal profiles and saved preferences.
  • 📜 Saved Recipe History – Auto-saved recipe archive for each user.
  • ❤️ Favorites List – Quickly bookmark your favorite dishes.
  • 📅 Recipe Scheduler – Schedule recipes to be sent to your email or phone.
  • 🧠 Smart Meal Planner – Weekly meal suggestions powered by AI.
  • 💾 Advanced Database Integration – Improved backend performance and dynamic data support.
